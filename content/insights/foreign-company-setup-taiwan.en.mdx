  - q: "Can a 100% foreign-owned company be set up in Taiwan?"
    a: "Yes, outside a small list of restricted industries. Most sectors accept 100% foreign ownership through the FIA (Foreign Investment Approval) channel."
  - q: "What's the minimum paid-in capital for a foreign-invested subsidiary?"
    a: "There's no universal minimum; it depends on industry and intended activities. Practical minimums to pass bank KYC and hire foreign staff usually range TWD 500,000 to TWD 2,000,000. Your specific industry licenses may set higher floors."
  - q: "Do foreign founders need to reside in Taiwan?"
    a: "No, but directors must provide notarized identity documents. Employment Gold Card or Entrepreneur Visa makes ongoing operations significantly easier."
